## Light Mode (Beach Theme)

### Background Colors
- **Sky Blue** (`#E3F2FD`) - Main app background (like a clear beach sky)
- **Sandy Beige** (`#FFF8E1`) - Navigation bar (warm sand)
- **White Surface** (95% opacity) - Cards and surfaces

### Primary Colors
- **Deep Ocean Blue** (`#4FC3F7`) - Primary buttons, active states
- **Seafoam Green** (`#B2DFDB`) - Secondary accents
- **Light Coral** (`#FFCCBC`) - Tertiary highlights

### Accent Colors
- **Soft Wave** (`#B3E5FC`) - Chips, containers
- **Sandy Gold** (`#FFE0B2`) - Warm accents
- **Beach Gray-Blue** (`#37474F`) - Text color

## Visual Style

The beach theme creates a calming, vacation-like atmosphere with:
- Very soft, pastel colors throughout
- Ocean-inspired blues and teals
- Warm sandy tones
- Minimal harsh contrasts
- Light, airy feel

## Dark Mode

Remains unchanged - deep space theme with orange and teal accents for consistency.

## Usage

Switch between themes in Settings ‚Üí Appearance ‚Üí Light/Dark Mode

The app will remember your preference!
